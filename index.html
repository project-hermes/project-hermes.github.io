We are monitoring the temperatures of the coastal ocean at depth worldwide in near real-time, combining satellite surface temperatures and diver measurements, using sensors ranging from precision instruments to dive computer profiles.

Straightforward? Yes
Simple? No

Weather Satellites

Satellites measure the temperature of the ocean's surface but not at depth. That's where the divers come in.

Divers

From all over the world divers collect detailed geolocated depth and temperature data. This is gathered from many sources such as the Hermes Sensor and dive computers.

Data

Through the open source cloud app all data is gathered into a centralized location. Making it simple for scientist, researchers or enthusiasts to make new discoveries about our ocean.
